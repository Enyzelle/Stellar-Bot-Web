Stellar Music Bot FAQ

Find answers to the most common questions about Stellar Music Bot

What is Stellar Music Bot?

Stellar Music Bot is a powerful, all-in-one Discord bot designed to enhance your server with music, fun, moderation, and utility commands.

How do I add Stellar Music Bot to my server?

You can add Stellar Music Bot to your server by clicking the invite link provided by the bot's developer or checking the official website.

What are the music commands available?

Stellar Music Bot offers a variety of music commands, including play, pause, skip, loop, and many more. For the full list, check the command list.

Does the bot support Spotify?

Yes, Stellar Music Bot supports Spotify links for tracks and playlists, along with YouTube and other popular platforms.

Who can use the moderation commands?

Moderation commands are restricted to users with appropriate server permissions, such as administrators or moderators.

How do I report an issue with the bot?

If you encounter any issues, please contact the bot developer or join the support server linked on the bot's page.
